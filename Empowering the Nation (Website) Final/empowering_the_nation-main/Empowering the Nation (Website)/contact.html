Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of courses do you offer?

We offer a variety of courses in leadership, personal development, and vocational skills to empower individuals.

2. How can I register for a course?

You can register for a course through our website by filling out the registration form or contacting us directly.

3. What are the payment options available?

We accept various payment options including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments at our venues.

4. Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for early registrations and group bookings. Please contact us for more details.

5. What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy allows for full refunds if cancellations are made at least 7 days before the course starts. Please refer to our website for more information.
